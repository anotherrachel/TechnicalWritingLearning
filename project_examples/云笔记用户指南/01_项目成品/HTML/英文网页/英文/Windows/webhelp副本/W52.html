Share to Group

Create a group before sharing.

  1. Click "+" on the leftmost bar.

  2. Create by clicking "Create Business Group".

  3. Input a name and click "create".

Group Basics

See basic functions for managing a group.

Add members.
  1. Right-click group name.

  2. Input email addresses and click "Add to Team" (see tips in the following page for 1 and 2).

Tips:
  • Click "Team" to fill in team information.
  • Click "Pricing" to buy advanced versions.
Now you can:
  1. Drag personal notes into the group folder and share with members.

  2. @ members in the header and share your note with them.

  3. Co-edit one group note with them.
