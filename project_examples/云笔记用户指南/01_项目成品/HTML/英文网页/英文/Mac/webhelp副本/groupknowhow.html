Group Basics

Some basic functions for managing group.

Add members

  1. Right-click a group name and select "Manage Team...".

  2. Add members by email.

  • Click "Team" to fill in team information.
  • Click "Pricing" to buy advanced versions.

Now you can:
  1. Drag personal notes into the group folder and share with group members.

  2. @ members in the header and share your note with them.

  3. Co-edit one group note with them.
